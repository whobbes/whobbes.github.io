This portfolio gives a quick selection of images that, for me, fit their purpose. Mostly documenting the bits of life I come across or, sometimes, playing with lights and shapes.

In my mind, the photograph is the most direct tool to share a point of view. Where the moving picture shows much and the text lets the imagination goes wild, the image sits in between. The game is to understand the embedded messages.

I started getting more into this medium fairly recently, now that the technology finally gives an answer on how to have a good photographic tool in a small package. I am influenced both by old days techniques as well as latest advancements. As always, I am eager to discover the future developments of this art form.
