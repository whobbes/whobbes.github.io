  • Hunter-Gatherers
    • Philippines - region of Palanan, Isabela
      Feb - Dec 2014

      Nearly a year of field work in a team of Anthropologists from UCL, London. The HG Resilience project aims at study the life of hunter-gatherers around the globe. In this part of it, we made a census of the region, conduct a detailed study on childcare, mobility of individuals between the camps and finally established an epidemiological profile of the population. Beyond all work, this immersion in such different culture and ways of life gave me a new approach on understanding life on planet Earth.
