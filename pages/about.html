Hi there, I am Wallace.

This website groups information on my background, creations and interests.

Broadly speaking, I have an telecommunications engineer vocation and a passion for artistic disciplines. I feel very lucky to be involved with technologies that can influence the daily life of many. With the shift of communication toward the internet, its future must be reinvented. Even the ways to access the web are in constant evolution. Following closely the latest developments on such technologies, I sometimes come up with a concept or two.

As for Arts, I am more specifically into the future of music and photography. While I love the classics, their purpose for me, is to better prepare for the next step. I was born when the world of image was mostly dependent on film and the music one on analog tools. The computer was nowhere to be found. It is now everywhere, used for nearly everything and can fit into a pocket or even under the skin, so what's next?

As a kid from the Alps, I love to ski in winter and climb in summer. Further afield you can find me scuba diving with turtles or more simply enjoying a five-a-side game at home.

I never turn down an occasion to travel, for work projects, special events or just to discover cultures and landscapes. From the simple wish of being fluent in English to spending nearly a year with hunter-gatherers, my passion for travelling has been growing ever since my first steps abroad.
